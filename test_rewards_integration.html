Manual Testing Guide

Follow these steps to manually test the rewards integration:

Login: Log in to your TaskHive account or create a new one.
Dashboard: Check that the rewards summary panel appears on your dashboard showing your balance and level.
View History: Click "View History" to open the rewards modal and verify it shows your transaction history.
Redemption Options: Verify that redemption options are visible in the rewards modal.
Leaderboard: Check that the leaderboard is visible in the rewards modal.
Create Task: Create a new task using the "Create New Task" button.
Accept Task: In another account, accept the task you just created.
Complete Task: Mark the task as completed in the account that accepted it.
Verify Task: Verify the task completion in your original account.
Check Rewards: Verify that rewards were automatically calculated and added to the account that completed the task.
